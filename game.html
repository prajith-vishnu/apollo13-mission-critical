ABOUT THIS SIMULATION // SOURCE RECORD

    
Every number that governs this simulation is taken from the historical
    record of Apollo 13 (April 11–17, 1970). This is not a story
    "inspired by" the mission — it is the mission's own arithmetic,
    running live:

    
Unlike historical archives that let you observe Apollo 13, this
    simulation puts you in command. Your decisions — not history's
    — determine if the crew survives.
